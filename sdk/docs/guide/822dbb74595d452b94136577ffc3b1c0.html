Usability

OpenUI5 implements usability features to address the requirements of people with special needs and to speed-up work with the application.

In this section:
  • Accessibility in OpenUI5
    Accessibility features are essential for users with disabilities. In an ongoing approach, OpenUI5 controls aim to comply with various product standards such as screen reader support, high-contrast theming and keyboard handling.
  • Advanced Accessibility Concepts in OpenUI5
    If you are developing OpenUI5-based applications, you have to be aware of the accessibility aspects of your application. A deeper understanding is needed, so that all accessibility requirements are met.
